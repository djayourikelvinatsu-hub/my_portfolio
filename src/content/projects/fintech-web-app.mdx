## Overview

Aura is a modern financial technology platform that simplifies personal wealth management. The project focused on delivering a premium, accessible, and blazingly fast user experience to build trust and simplify complex financial data.

## Key Highlights

- **Bespoke Animations**: Extensive use of `framer-motion` for fluid page transitions, micro-interactions, and engaging onboarding flows.
- **Data Visualization**: Custom, interactive charts built with `Chart.js` to visualize spending habits and investment growth over time.
- **Accessibility First**: Ensured WCAG AA compliance across the entire application, including keyboard navigation and screen reader support for complex data tables.

## Performance Focus

A significant effort was placed on optimizing the application's perceived performance. Assets were aggressively preloaded, and dynamic imports were utilized to keep the initial JavaScript payload under 80KB.
