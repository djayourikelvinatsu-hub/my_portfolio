## Overview

MediTrack Pro is an advanced hospital management platform built to optimize daily healthcare operations and active patient tracking. The application leverages a real-time WebSocket architecture to deliver critical medical alerts instantly to staff, ensuring rapid responses to urgent patient needs.

## Features

- **Real-Time Notifications:** Integrated a Socket.IO server on the backend to emit critical alerts instantly, displayed via responsive UI toasts to medical staff.
- **Patient Management:** Enhanced patient list views providing quick access to essential medical tracking, history, and status.
- **Secure Access:** Robust authentication and role-based management to ensure sensitive healthcare data remains protected and compliant.
