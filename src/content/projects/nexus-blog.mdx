## Overview

Nexus Blog is a contemporary blogging platform designed to deliver the latest news and insights in the technology and business sectors. The application offers a seamless reading experience across all devices, featuring a clean, minimalist user interface with support for both light and dark modes.

## Features

- **Responsive Design:** Optimized layouts providing an excellent reading experience on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.
- **Modern Architecture:** Built with React, TypeScript, and Vite for fast performance and a robust development experience.
- **Dynamic Theming:** Seamless switching between light and dark modes to suit reader preferences (powered by next-themes).
- **Sleek Aesthetics:** Styled with Tailwind CSS to ensure a clean, accessible, and consistent visual language throughout the platform.
